Descrete Mathematics

Description Descrete Mathematics is the study of mathematical structures that are fundamentally discrete rather than continuous.Descrete objects can be enumerated by integers. More formally descrete mathematics has been characterized as the branch of mathematics dealing with countable sets.Concepts in descrete mathematics are useful in studying and describing objects and problems in the branches like computer science.
